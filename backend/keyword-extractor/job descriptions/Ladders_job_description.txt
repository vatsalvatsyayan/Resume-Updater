About the job

For our client, we are seeking a Software Engineer to join the team of a leader in the Enterprise Technology space. As a Software Engineer, you’ll play a key role driving outcomes across the Enterprise Technology organization. You’ll collaborate with cross-functional partners to solve meaningful problems and deliver results. This role offers visibility, ownership, and the opportunity to make a measurable impact.

Location: Remote

Compensation: $138,700 – $203,900 annually.

Responsibilities


 Design and implement scalable backend services using Java, Scala, and Python
 Develop frontend interfaces using React and Node.js
 Collaborate with product managers during product discovery to align solutions with business needs
 Lead API design to improve system integration and performance
 Manage cloud services to ensure high availability and fault tolerance
 Optimize database interactions for high throughput
 Process and analyze over 1 billion price transactions daily


Qualifications


 Strong proficiency in programming languages such as Java, or similar
 Strong understanding of API development frameworks and backend development
 Experience with distributed cloud services and microservices architecture
 Solid knowledge of database management and application development
 Excellent problem-solving skills under tight deadlines
 Strong communication skills with both technical and non-technical stakeholders


Benefits


 Generous time-off and wellness leave
 Healthcare coverage
 Retirement savings program
 Support for volunteering and donation efforts


Our client is an equal opportunity employer. We encourage you to apply even if you don’t meet every qualification—your background could be exactly what this team needs.
