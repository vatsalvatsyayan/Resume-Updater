About the job

Responsibilities

Kforce has a client in Miami, FL that is seeking a hands-on Developer with a focus on automation and AI tools to help streamline workflows and improve productivity across the organization. This role involves designing Python-based scripts, leveraging platforms like Microsoft Copilot and ChatGPT, and collaborating with teams to deliver efficient, scalable automation solutions. Key Responsibilities:


 Develop and maintain Python scripts to automate workflows, data processes, and routine tasks
 Integrate and apply AI tools (Microsoft Copilot, ChatGPT, etc.) to support development, documentation, and internal productivity
 Partner with business and technical teams to identify automation opportunities and translate requirements into practical solutions
 Troubleshoot, debug, and optimize scripts and automation pipelines
 Document code, processes, and provide knowledge transfer to team members


Requirements


 Strong proficiency in Python with proven experience in scripting and automation
 Familiarity with AI/automation tools (e.g., Microsoft Copilot, ChatGPT, or similar)
 Experience writing clean, maintainable code and debugging effectively
 Ability to communicate technical solutions clearly to both technical and non-technical stakeholders
 Strong organizational skills and attention to detail


Preferred Qualifications


 Experience working with APIs, automation platforms (e.g., Zapier, Power Automate), or cloud environments
 Background in IT, operations, or data analysis
 Knowledge of prompt engineering or conversational AI workflows
 Demonstrated curiosity and enthusiasm for emerging technologies
