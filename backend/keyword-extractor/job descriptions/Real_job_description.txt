About the job

We have a current opportunity for a Systems Engineer on a contract basis at a leading medical device company. The position will be based in Arden Hills, MN. See key details below:





Required


Bachelor's degree in Engineering, Computer Science, Information Systems, or a related field.
3+ years of experience working with system configuration, project setup, or technical tooling support.
Familiarity with requirement tracking platforms, configuration or version-control systems, and issue‑management tools.
Ability to manage documentation updates, change requests, and structured project records.
Experience supporting testing or system readiness activities.
Strong attention to detail and organizational skills.
Ability to work across teams and communicate clearly in a technical environment.


Preferred


Experience in a regulated or compliance‑focused industry.
Exposure to security reviews, risk considerations, or change‑impact assessments.
Background working with multiple interconnected engineering or lifecycle tools.
Strong analytical skills and comfort navigating structured technical information.


If you're interested in this opportunity, we encourage you to apply and connect with us.
