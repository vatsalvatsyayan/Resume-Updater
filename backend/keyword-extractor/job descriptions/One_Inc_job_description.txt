About the job

Software Engineer Level I (AI & Agentic Systems)

Location: US Remote

 

Overview:

As a Software Engineer Level I, you will support the development of modern software systems, including AI-powered applications. You’ll work closely with senior engineers to build and maintain full-stack components, contribute to AI agent development, and grow your skills in a collaborative, fast-paced environment.

 

Key Responsibilities:

 Assist in the development and maintenance of software components, including backend and frontend features
Collaborate with senior engineers to implement AI-based agents and integrate LLM APIs
Participate in the software development lifecycle, including design, development, testing, and deployment
Write clean, maintainable code and contribute to code reviews
Troubleshoot and debug issues in development and staging environments
Learn and apply best practices in software engineering, version control, and containerized environments
Research and experiment with new tools and technologies under guidance

 

Skills: 

 Basic understanding of programming principles and object-oriented design
Familiarity with Python (required); experience with JavaScript/TypeScript or web development is a plus
Exposure to or interest in AI technologies, especially LLM APIs (e.g., OpenAI, Claude) or agentic systems is a plus
Understanding of HTML, CSS, and basic frontend concepts is helpful
Familiarity with Git or other version control systems
Strong problem-solving and learning mindset
Ability to work independently with guidance and collaborate effectively in a remote team

 

Education & Experience:

Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science or a related field, or currently pursuing one
1+ years of professional software development experience
Any academic, personal, or internship experience with AI/ML or LLMs is a plus

 

 

Desired Traits:

Curious, proactive, and eager to grow. You take ownership of your work, ask thoughtful questions, and are excited to learn from experienced engineers. You’re adaptable, detail-oriented, and passionate about building impactful technology.
