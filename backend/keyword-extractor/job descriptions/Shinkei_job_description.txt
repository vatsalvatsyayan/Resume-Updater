About the job

What We Do:

Shinkei Systems uses robotics to build a world where fishermen thrive, supply chains are transparent, and American food systems restore national vitality.




Our technology integrates computer vision and advanced mechanisms to assist fishermen in fish processing, enhancing efficiency and product quality.




What You Get to Do:

You will be working with a fast moving team of software and mechanical engineers during the most exciting phase of our growth – expanding to support new fish species, more robots in the field, and developing software based tools for fishermen efficiency!
Design, build, and test critical components and services for our fish processing system
Own the execution and testing of software features from development to deployment
Collaborate in a multi disciplinary team to improve robot behavior, sensor integration, and data pipelines
Investigate failure modes while robots are deployed onboard fishermen boats




What You Will Need: 

A bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Robotics, or a related field
Experience owning a significant project in C++ or Python
Passion for developing clean and organized software and testing methods
Strong problem-solving skills and an ability to work collaboratively in a fast-paced, highly cross-functional setting
Excellent written and verbal communication skills
Desire to learn new technologies and to apply them to solve real-world challenges
