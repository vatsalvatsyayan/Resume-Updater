About the job

Are you ready to help shape the digital future of a leading global staffing agency? Aquent is seeking a Full-Stack Web Engineer who thrives on building robust, user-centric web applications. You’ll be part of a collaborative technology group that powers innovation across internal operations, empowers talent, and enhances the client experience.

In this role, you’ll work across the full software development lifecycle—from conceptual design to deployment and ongoing optimization. You’ll collaborate closely with cross-functional teams in product, design, research, QA, and project management to develop scalable, secure, and impactful digital solutions.

If you’re passionate about building full-stack web solutions, contributing to a culture of continuous improvement, and making meaningful impact through technology, this is your opportunity to thrive.

What You’ll Be Doing


Participate in Agile planning sessions, design reviews, and team retrospectives. 
Contribute to the architecture and development of scalable web applications. 
Design and implement end-user features across the full stack: database, server-side, and front-end. 
Maintain and optimize existing web applications; identify and resolve bugs efficiently. 
Ensure robust data structures and integrity through thoughtful schema design and migrations. 
Build secure integrations with third-party services and APIs. 
Apply secure coding practices and stay informed of web security best practices. 
Conduct peer code reviews and contribute to version control systems (Git). 
Manage and update user stories/tasks in project management tools. 
Collaborate with QA and support teams to debug and resolve issues. 
Implement automated tests to maintain software quality and reliability. 
Demonstrate new features and gather feedback from stakeholders. 


What You’ll Bring

Technical Expertise


Experience building and consuming REST APIs. 
Familiarity with templating frameworks (e.g., JSP, Thymeleaf). 
Understanding of MVC and SOA architectural patterns. 
Strong JavaScript skills and experience with front-end frameworks (e.g., Angular, jQuery). 
Solid grasp of HTML5, CSS/LESS/SASS, and AJAX patterns. 
Proficiency in working with JSON, XML, and HTTP protocols. 
Hands-on experience with source control systems (e.g., Git). 
Familiarity with code quality tools and continuous integration pipelines. 


Personal Attributes


Collaborative mindset and strong team orientation. 
Excellent written and verbal communication skills. 
Commitment to learning, growth, and process improvement. 
Curiosity, initiative, and a holistic approach to problem-solving. 


Education & Experience


Formal training or self-directed learning in full-stack web development. 
Proven ability to build meaningful, scalable web applications—whether through work experience, internships, or personal projects.
