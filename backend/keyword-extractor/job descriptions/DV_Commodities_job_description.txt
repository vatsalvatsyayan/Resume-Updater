About the job

About Us:

Founded more than 15 years ago and headquartered in Chicago, the DV Group of financial services firms has grown to more than 450 people operating throughout North America and in Europe. Since spinning out of a large brokerage firm in 2016, DV Trading has rapidly scaled as an independent proprietary trading firm utilizing its own capital, trading strategies, and risk management methodologies to provide liquidity to worldwide financial markets and hedging opportunities to commodity producers and users. Now, DV group affiliates include two broker dealers, a cryptocurrency market making firm, and a bourgeoning investment adviser.




DV Commodities is a division within DV Group that specializes in trading commodities markets, including crude oil, refined products, natural gas, power, emissions, metals, softs, and agriculture. DV Commodities' risk management, world-class technology, and talented traders have enabled it to become one of the world’s largest liquidity providers and risk allocators in the energy and commodities spaces. DV Commodities currently has over 35 desks, with traders and staff located in London, New York, Chicago Houston, and Dubai.




Responsibilities:

Build and improve a variety of proprietary trading tools to support the trading desks across the business
Develop and maintain full-stack services and web applications (front-end, back-end) for Futures, Options and Derivatives trading
Collaborate with other developers, tech leaders, traders, and researchers to outline the technical needs for each trading application
Design, build, and deploy responsive and scalable applications for trading, research and live market data streaming using modern technologies.
Work with technology and business stakeholders in an iterative fashion to deliver platform functionality




Requirements:

Degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or related field
Experience programming in either C++, Python, or Java in Linux environments
Excellent analytical and creative problem-solving skills




Annual compensation range $120K - $150K + discretionary bonus eligibility
