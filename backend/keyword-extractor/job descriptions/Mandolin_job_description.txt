About the job

About Mandolin

Nearly every disease will become treatable in our lifetimes. Mandolin is laying the clinical and financial infrastructure to get groundbreaking treatments to patients faster, powered by AI agents.

Mandolin partners closely with the largest healthcare institutions in the US, covering more than $10B drug spend across the country. We're backed by Greylock, SV Angel, Maverick, SignalFire, and the founders of Vercel, Decagon, and Yahoo.

Why we need you

We’re building a new Engineering team with a clear mission: to seamlessly integrate Mandolin into our customers’ systems and ensure every new partnership starts strong. Think of this as a launchpad—where you’ll gain deep, hands-on experience with our product, technology, team, and customers. It’s a chance to build foundational knowledge that can grow into a long-term path within Integrations or across other parts of Mandolin as you expand your impact. We’re looking for curious, driven engineers eager to dive in and start building.

What You’ll Do


Designing and implementing integration solutions to connect disparate systems, applications, and platforms
Collaborating with cross-functional teams to gather and analyze system requirements
Developing and maintaining system integration architecture and documentation
Conducting integration testing and troubleshooting to ensure system compatibility and stability
Monitoring system performance and implementing improvements to enhance functionality
Configuring and maintaining middleware and other integration tools
Providing technical support and guidance for integration-related issues to IT teams and stakeholders
Staying current with industry trends and emerging technologies to drive innovation in integration practices
Creating and managing APIs for system interaction and data exchange

Excellent communicator with strong relationship-building skills
