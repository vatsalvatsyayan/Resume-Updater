About Workerbee

Workerbee is the AI-powered platform for trusted, modern hiring.

 

We connect companies with the right mix of enterprise-proven experts and AI-savvy specialists; full-time, part-time, or project-based. 




Our intelligent, multi-agent system deeply understands each specialist and every customer need, enabling better-fit matches and faster results.

 

We bring together people and intelligent systems to make hiring faster, smarter, and more human.




We have customers in need of Junior Back End Development talent.




By joining Workerbee you can be matched for project-based, contract, or permanent opportunities with leading organizations.




As a Workerbee:

You will help solve complex problems and bring real value to clients through hands-on expertise in backend development and application architecture.




What our customers need help with:

Building and maintaining backend services using languages such as Python, Java, Node.js, or Go
Developing RESTful APIs and database integrations
Supporting cloud-based applications and scalable infrastructure
Writing clean, maintainable code following industry best practices
Collaborating with frontend and DevOps teams to deliver production features




What stands out amongst top talent:

Strong understanding of backend fundamentals including data structures and APIs
Familiarity with relational and NoSQL databases
Experience using Git and collaborative development workflows
Awareness of security best practices in web development
Eagerness to learn and adapt within fast-paced engineering teams




Why Join Workerbee

Access: Work on high-impact projects with leading companies across North America
Community: Join a trusted network of specialists and professionals in your field
Growth: Expand your skills through exclusive learning and development opportunities
Flexibility: Choose projects that fit your schedule and work style
