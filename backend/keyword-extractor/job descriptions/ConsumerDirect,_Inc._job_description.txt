About the job

Reports to: Software Engineer Team Lead

***This position is full-time and in person located in Irvine, CA***




About ConsumerDirect

At ConsumerDirect, we’re transforming the way consumers control their credit, money and privacy through innovative software solutions. As one of Orange County’s Best Places to Work for four years running, we pride ourselves on a collaborative, inclusive, and growth-focused culture. Join our passionate team of professionals dedicated to making a real impact in the financial technology space. As we continue to scale and innovate, we’re looking for a talented individual to join us in our mission to revolutionize the industries we serve.




Position Overview:

We are seeking a Software Engineer II to join our engineering team and contribute to the design, development, and maintenance of scalable, API-driven applications. This role focuses on hands-on development, collaboration, and continuous improvement of our systems while providing opportunities to grow technically and professionally. 




You will be expected to take ownership of assigned tickets, research solutions within our existing technology stack, and continuously improve your skills while contributing feedback to the development process. 




Job duties:

Develop, test, and maintain software using Java and Spring-based technologies
Implement features according to designs and technical direction provided by the team lead
Follow ticket requirements and engineering standards when delivering work
Research and propose solutions within the constraints of the existing architecture and tech stack
Participate in code reviews and apply feedback to improve code quality
Troubleshoot and resolve application and production issues
Consistently deliver assigned tasks in a timely manner
Ask questions and seek clarification to ensure high-quality outcomes
Provide constructive feedback on development processes and tooling 




Qualifications:

Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field (or equivalent experience) (required)
At least 3 years of professional software development experience (required)
Working proficiency in Java and Spring (mid-level competency)
Experience building and supporting RESTful APIs and backend services
Familiarity with relational databases and ORM frameworks (e.g., Hibernate)
Ability to follow established designs while independently implementing solutions
Strong problem-solving and research skills within an existing codebase
Experience using Git for version control
Experience with AWS services (e.g., EC2, SQS, RDS)
Familiarity with messaging systems such as JMS
Exposure to CI/CD pipelines and tools such as Jenkins
Experience working with Jira or similar ticketing systems
Familiarity with Tomcat or similar application servers




Working Conditions & Physical Requirements

Office environment with prolonged computer and screen use
Requires sitting for extended periods and regular keyboard and mouse use
