About the job

Job Description

Our Opportunity:

Chewy is seeking a Software Engineer to join an established technology team in Boston, MA or Belllevue, WA. At Chewy, it is our mission to be the most trusted and convenient destination for pet parents (and partners) everywhere. The prospective candidate will have the opportunity to work with a talented group of backend and frontend engineers, product managers, and business leaders in an Agile environment, develop impactful software capabilities to enhance or create new functionality needed to grow Chewy. In this role, the ideal candidate will engage in development, testing, and make contributions to the growth and development of one of Chewy’s fastest growing areas. They will have the opportunity to be part of a high performing engineering team where we value broad-based technical skills.

What You’ll Do


Actively participate in the definition, design, development, testing and implementation of software functionality using an agile methodology
Partner with stakeholders and technology teams on how best to implement desired features and enhancement
Actively participate in architecture and code reviews to deliver high quality code
Collaborate to identify, isolate, and resolve malfunctions related to software
Establish strong working relationships at all organizational levels and across functional teams
Take responsibility and ownership of project timelines and deliverables
Participate in on-call rotations, troubleshooting production issues and contribute to operational excellence


What You’ll Need


Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Computer Information Systems, or similar; or equivalent work experience
1+ years of Software Engineering experience.
Experience developing software componets and/or services by use of the following technologies: Java/Kotlin, NextJS, Terraform
Experience with building, deploying, testing APIs and micro-services
Knowledge of professional software engineering practices for the full software development life cycle including coding standards, code reviews, source control management, build processes, testing and operations
Demonstrated ability to effectively communicate with stakeholders
Must be team oriented and have a customer first mindset


Bonus (if Applicable)


Experience with agile methodologies a plus
Experience with cloud & orchestration technologies (AWS Services, Terraform, Ansible etc)
Knowledge/experience of Search Engine Optimization (SEO) practices


The base salary range for this role is $103,000 - $164,500.


The specific salary offered to a candidate may be influenced by a variety of factors including but not limited to the candidate’s relevant experience, education, and work location. In addition, this position is eligible for 401k and a new hire and annual equity grant. C08+ positions may also be eligible for annual bonus.


We offer different types of insurance and benefits, such as medical/Rx, vision, dental, life, disability, hospital indemnity, critical illness, and accident. We offer parental leave, family services benefits, backup dependent care, flexible spending accounts, telemedicine, pet adoption reimbursement, employee assistance program, and many discounts including 10% off pet insurance and 20% off at Chewy.com.

Exempt salary team members have unlimited PTO, subject to manager approval. Team members will receive six paid holidays per year. Team members may be eligible for paid sick and family leave in compliance with applicable state and local regulations.
