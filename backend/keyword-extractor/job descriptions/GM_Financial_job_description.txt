Responsibilities

About the Role:

As a Software Development Engineer (SDE) I-Gen AI, you’ll be a key contributor on an Agile Scrum team, working across all areas of product development—from building new features to integrating cutting-edge technologies. While you may bring strengths in specific tools or languages, we value adaptability and a growth mindset. You’ll have opportunities to expand your skill set, collaborate across domains, and help the team deliver high-impact solutions by stepping in wherever needed to meet shared goals.


Design and deploy generative AI applications and solutions, consistently delivering high-quality results aligned with team goals.
Build scalable ML pipelines and data workflows in Databricks, applying robust software development practices within Agile Scrum teams.
Integrate Azure AI services into enterprise applications, contribute to cloud migration efforts, and accelerate implementation through continuous learning and experimentation.
Develop APIs and microservices for AI model serving, maintaining a production-first mindset focused on scalability, uptime, and rapid issue resolution.
Implement Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems, enhancing contextual relevance and performance of LLM-based solutions.
Create custom AI solutions using LLMs and foundation models, supporting product features across web applications, data platforms, and API services.
Establish monitoring and evaluation frameworks for AI systems, leveraging automated pipelines to improve deployment speed and quality.
Collaborate with data scientists to productionize ML models, contribute to test automation, peer reviews, and continuous improvement of product quality.
Invest in Agile practices, improving time to market, team collaboration, and overall delivery excellence
