Core4ce is hiring a Junior Full Stack Developer with experience of server-side and front-end languages. This position supports the Expeditionary Medicine Platform Augmentation Readiness and Training System (EMPARTS) that is leveraged by the Bureau of Navy Medicine (BUMED)

Responsibilities


Complete end-to-end solutions in a fast-paced agile environment
Provide full life cycle process-oriented software development and integration
Design, development, and implementation of software components from vision to production-ready system
Analyze, implement, schedule, test, and deploy full features and components of solutions for the better
Collaborate as a team-member with an existing, established group of teammates
Participate in the evolution of the system architecture towards new capabilities
This position is designed to be flexible, with responsibilities evolving to meet business needs and enable individual growth.


Requirements


1-2 years of experience with Full Stack Development
Ability to obtain DoD Secret Clearance
Experience using an Agile environment
Experience with front-end frame works such as Angular or React
Proficiency with fundamental front-end languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
Proficiency in server-side languages such as Python, Java, Node.js, and ASP.NET
Experience using debugging and observability tolls for live systems
Experience working with teammates collaborating in a modern CI/CD pipeline
Experience with tooling such as JIRA, Git, etc.


Preferred


Prior Federal Experience
Degree in Computer Science
DoD 8570.1-M Compliance at IAT Level I Security + Preferred
