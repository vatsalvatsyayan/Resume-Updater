About the job

nCino offers exciting career opportunities for individuals who want to join the worldwide leader in cloud banking.

As a Software Engineer on nCino's Portfolio Analytics team, you'll develop full-stack solutions that help financial institutions make data-driven decisions. Working with TypeScript on the front end while leveraging AWS infrastructure and Java services, you'll build user-facing features that transform complex financial data into intuitive, actionable tools.

You'll collaborate with experienced engineers through code reviews and technical discussions while gradually taking on more complex features across the entire stack. Your day-to-day work involves building new capabilities from the ground up, shaping product direction through hands-on development, and contributing to an emerging fintech solution within nCino's established platform. This opportunity offers the combination of startup energy with enterprise stability. Grow your full-stack development skills with modern web technologies and cloud platforms all while making meaningful contributions to innovative fintech solutions from day one.
