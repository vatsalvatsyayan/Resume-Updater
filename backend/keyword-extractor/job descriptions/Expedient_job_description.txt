About the job

Summary

Join Expedient's AI CTRL product team to build a secure, powerful enterprise AI platform that combines enterprise data with agentic AI systems. As an AI Software Engineer, you'll contribute across the full product lifecycle—from platform development to client support and custom integrations

Key Responsibilities

Develop and maintain the AI CTRL platform, including backend services, APIs, and frontend components
Troubleshoot technical issues by analyzing logs, debugging code, and working with clients and support teams
Build integrations with external systems and create custom automated workflows
Collaborate across teams, pivoting between projects as priorities evolve
Write robust, scalable code while championing AI-powered development tools
Participate in the complete software development lifecycle from design to deployment
Qualifications:
Experience: Strong software engineering foundation with experience across multiple domains (backend, frontend, DevOps, APIs).  Ability to learn new technologies quickly and thrive in fast-paced environments
Technical Skills: Proficiency in Python, Go, Java, or JavaScript.  Understanding of cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP, VMware).  Knowledge of CI/CD, RESTful APIs, containerization (Docker, Kubernetes), and databases (SQL, NoSQL). Familiarity with or strong interest in AI/ML technologies
Key Attributes: Exceptionally adaptable with strong problem-solving skills.  Self-motivated with a proactive, ownership mindset.  Excellent team player committed to customer success.  Passion for building impactful technology
Education: Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or related field, OR equivalent practical experience

Location: Cleveland, Ohio office.  On-site role, regional travel may be required.

Salary for this position will be based on your experience and your skills.  Estimated salary range is $100,000 to $125,000 annually.
