We're seeking a motivated individual with a strong foundation in computer science, or related field, who is ready to contribute to a fast-paced environment and help us build better company cultures.

What you will do:


Work as part of a team to plan and implement software solutions that help drive business culture and success
Design and implement new software features and maintain existing ones to ensure reliability and performance
Participate in making team commitments and collaborating to deliver on them within an Agile framework
Adhere to company architectural standards and software development best practices
Write comprehensive unit and integration tests and participate in quality assurance efforts
Engage in peer code reviews, willingly incorporating team feedback and providing thoughtful feedback to others
Write code that is easy to understand and can be considered self-documenting, providing additional documentation when necessary
Involve technical leadership in the solution design process where appropriate to ensure scalability and quality
Keep up to date with new technologies and industry trends to continuously improve your skills
Help in the recruiting and hiring of engineering team members
