Position

Flight Ops Full-Stack Software Engineer

Location: 

34 Parker, Irvine, CA

Position Description

DZYNE Technologies is seeking a Full-Stack Web Application Developer to design and build internal tools that streamline operations and improve team workflows. You will embed with our Flight Operations team and observe their manual workflows to discover points ready for automation. From these observations, you will develop web-based systems that make complex processes simple and efficient — from data integration to real-time dashboards — helping teams move faster and work smarter. You will create clean, intuitive front-end interfaces and build robust back-end APIs and databases. The ideal candidate is a developer who loves quickly addressing real-world needs with elegant, maintainable software.

Responsibilities


Design and develop internal web applications and dashboards to support flight operations, ground check out, and system testing.
Collaborate closely with cross-functional teams to understand workflows and pain points to rapidly prototype solutions.
Build, test, and deploy full-stack applications using modern front- and backend frameworks (React, Node, Postgres or other databases).
Design and maintain secure, scalable, and maintainable codebases.


Required Skills/Qualifications


Strong proficiency in JavaScript, React, and full-stack web development.
Experience building RESTful APIs and working with relational databases (e.g., PostgreSQL).
Familiarity with CI/CD pipelines and agile tools.
Familiarity with virtual machines and cloud platforms for hosting
Familiarity with Docker deployment
Comfort working in fast-paced environments, both in and on-site, with evolving requirements.


Preferred Skills/Qualifications


Experience integrating software tools with real-time data or hardware systems.
Gitlab CI/CD pipeline scripting
Exposure to DevOps best practices
Experience working with teams that operate physical systems (IoT, robotics, or similar)
Drone software exposure


Education


Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or related field (or equivalent experience)

DZYNE Technologies is a leading innovator in autonomous defense solutions, specializing in the design and development of advanced unmanned aerial systems (UAS) and counter-UAS technologies. Our flagship product, the Unmanned Long-endurance Tactical Reconnaissance Aircraft (ULTRA), exemplifies our commitment to cutting-edge innovation. Our Dronebuster DTIM kit, a compact, lightweight, and portable system that integrates detection, tracking, identification, and mitigation capabilities. This American-made solution is designed for tactical flexibility and operational adaptability, providing operators with real-time defense against evolving drone threats.

Additionally, our Sawtooth platform delivers critical infrastructure defense through high-performing sensors and effectors specifically optimized for counter-UAS missions. Regularly updated to address rapidly evolving threats, Sawtooth ensures robust and adaptable protection for a variety of operational environments.
