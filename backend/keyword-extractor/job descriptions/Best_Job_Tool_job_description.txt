About The Role

We are seeking a highly skilled Senior Software Engineer to join our team in Hazelwood, MO. This pivotal role involves supporting the Air Force's Dynamic Mission Planning/Replanning initiatives, which are crucial for national defense. The successful candidate will contribute to the development of advanced capabilities that enable real-time dynamic mission replanning (DMR) within a classified cloud environment accessed via mobile technology. This position offers an exciting opportunity to work on innovative solutions that will support and enhance our national security posture for decades to come. As part of Boeing's esteemed team, you will leverage your expertise in software development, system architecture, and agile methodologies to deliver impactful results in a challenging and rewarding environment.

Qualifications

The ideal candidate will possess a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field, along with a minimum of 9 years of experience in software development. Proficiency in coding languages such as C#, .NET, and REACT is essential, coupled with experience using version control tools like Git or Bitbucket. Familiarity with modern software development patterns including microservices, RESTful APIs, mono repositories, and single-page applications (SPAs) is required. Candidates should have a strong background in designing and implementing system architectures, requirements gathering, software testing, and debugging. Experience with Typescript, ReactJS/Redux, and working within large-scale Agile teams is highly desirable. The ability to obtain a U.S. Secret Security Clearance is mandatory, and U.S. citizenship is required for employment.

Responsibilities


Support the design, development, testing, and maintenance of software throughout its lifecycle, ensuring high quality and reliability.
Collaborate with the Product Owner to review, analyze, and translate customer requirements into effective software designs.
Develop, maintain, and enhance software products and functionalities, focusing on system integration and performance optimization.
Create and maintain architecture documentation, requirements, algorithms, interfaces, and design specifications for software solutions.
Debug and resolve technical issues promptly to ensure system stability and efficiency.
Troubleshoot complex technical problems, providing innovative solutions to support mission-critical operations.
Contribute to the development and continuous improvement of software processes and best practices within the team.
