About the Role

We are looking for a talented and experienced Software Developer experienced in developing backend services using cloud native technologies. As a member of this team, you will design, build, and maintain scalable and high-performance backend services. You will work closely with the product, frontend, and DevOps teams to deliver robust solutions that power our cutting-edge products that run in the cloud and the edge.

 

Location. This role is office-based at our Bellevue, Washington office. 

 

What You'll Do (Key Responsibilities)

Design, develop, and maintain efficient, reusable, and reliable code, preferably using Go (Golang).
Build and enhance SDKs and APIs to facilitate customer integration and interaction with the marketplace platform.
Collaborate with cross-functional teams (frontend, product, DevOps) to define, design, and ship new features.
Optimize applications for maximum speed and scalability, ensuring high availability and responsiveness.
Write unit tests and integration tests to ensure code quality.
Identify bottlenecks and bugs and devise solutions to these problems.
Work with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, Azure) and deploy applications in containerized environments (Docker, Kubernetes).
Maintain code documentation and architecture design records.
Participate in code reviews, providing constructive feedback to peers.

 

Required Qualifications:

2+ years of backend development experience, preferably with Go (Golang).
Experience building SDKs and APIs for public marketplace use.
Proven experience in designing and implementing CI/CD pipelines for Kubernetes deployments.
Familiarity with SQL/NoSQL databases (e.g., MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Redis).
Knowledge of concurrency in Go, and an understanding of Go routines and channels.
Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, Azure) and working in distributed cloud-native environments.
Proficiency in version control systems (Git).
Familiarity with containerization using Docker and Kubernetes.
Experience with unit testing, integration testing, and performance tuning.
