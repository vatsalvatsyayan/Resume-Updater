About the job

Associate Software Developer | 2601 W Plane Rest Dr, Waukegan | On-site | $80,000 - $90,000




We're working with Uline on this exciting opportunity.




Unlock your potential with Uline's LearnIT program! This isn't just a job; it's a launching pad for your software development career, offering 6 weeks of intensive hands-on training in Java, Spring, and Angular to build innovative applications and shape the future of a booming industry leader.




The Role




• Design and implement cutting-edge Java applications using Spring and Angular frameworks.

• Create and maintain dynamic user interface patterns and a robust standard library.

• Troubleshoot and resolve software issues, ensuring top-tier code quality through comprehensive unit testing.

• Collaborate with business analysts to translate requirements into powerful technical solutions.




What You'll Need




• Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, IT, or a related technical field.

• Educational experience in C#, Java, or other object-oriented languages, alongside strong SQL programming skills.

• Proven experience interacting with and building REST APIs.

• A solid understanding of object-oriented principles and relational database design.




What's On Offer




• Competitive health insurance, a 401(k) with a 6% employer match from day one, and multiple bonus programs.

• Generous paid time off and holidays to help you recharge and thrive.

• Access to an on-site fitness center, walking paths, and modern facilities.
