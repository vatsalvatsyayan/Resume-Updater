Coinbase is a leading cryptocurrency exchange and financial technology company committed to increasing economic freedom worldwide. As a pioneer in the blockchain and crypto space, Coinbase provides a secure, user-friendly platform that enables individuals and institutions to buy, sell, and manage digital assets. About The Role

We are seeking a talented Software Engineer to join our dynamic team. In this role, you will be instrumental in developing, testing, and deploying product-facing features that are critical to our platform's performance, security, and scalability. You will collaborate closely with cross-functional teams including product managers, operations, and other engineers to design and implement solutions that address complex technical challenges. Your work will directly impact the functionality of key components such as market data, trading systems, authentication, and security protocols. We value individuals who are passionate about blockchain technology and are eager to contribute to the evolution of the financial ecosystem. This role offers an exciting opportunity to leave a tangible mark on the future of digital finance while working in a fast-paced, innovative environment.
