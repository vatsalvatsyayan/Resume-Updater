About the job

What To Expect
The Tesla Energy UI team is seeking a skilled Full-Stack Software Engineer to join our mission of accelerating the world’s transition to sustainable energy. You will develop customer-facing tools that empower users to understand the value and technical capabilities of Megapacks during the sales stage. Using TypeScript, React, and high-performance backend distributed systems, you’ll build scalable, data-driven user interfaces that drive Megapack sales and shape a sustainable future. Your work will directly impact the energy industry by enabling intuitive, visually compelling tools that enhance customer experiences and operational efficiency.

You’ll collaborate closely with customers, product managers, and cross-functional teams to deliver extensible design systems and open-source quality solutions. Our fast-paced, entrepreneurial environment encourages rapid iteration and innovation, where your passion for engineering groundbreaking energy solutions will thrive. Expect to tackle complex challenges, contribute to a dynamic team culture, and create delightful user interactions that redefine how energy systems are presented and sold.

This role requires a high degree of initiative, creativity, and technical expertise. You will be expected to work collaboratively with the sales and engineering teams, as well as independently on projects and initiatives as needed.

What You'll Do


Contribute to the complete software development lifecycle for Energy UI applications, ensuring that all projects are delivered on time and meet high-quality standards
Design, develop, and maintain full stack web applications, utilizing React, TypeScript, GraphQL and web tooling such as Next.js to create highly performant and scalable user experiences
Build, deploy, and scale web systems ranging from sites to middleware APIs using Git, Docker, Kubernetes, AWS and other relevant DevOps technologies
Collaborate with teams to create and implement world-class UI/UX designs using Figma, ensuring accurate code implementation
Work with cross-functional teams, including product managers, sales engineers, business stakeholders and other software engineers, to define and implement features that enhance operational efficiency for our customers
Ensure that our web applications integrate seamlessly with backend distributed systems and support real-time monitoring of all applications owned by the team
Contribute to the definition and implementation of extensible design systems that prioritize scalability, maintainability, and exceptional user experiences
Implement rigorous testing strategies, including unit, integration, and end-to-end testing, to guarantee the reliability and performance of applications under various conditions
Actively maintain and promote the core values of the team, which include engineering excellence, curiosity, a bias for action, self-awareness, inclusivity, and vulnerability, contributing to a positive and productive team culture



What You'll Bring


Proven experience as a Full Stack Software Engineer with a strong focus on developing and delivering user-centric web applications using React, TypeScript, GraphQL, and Next.js
Solid understanding of web development best practices, design patterns, and scalability principles, including strategies for component reuse, such as module federation
Strong problem-solving and analytical skills, with the ability to understand complex business problems and design software solutions to address them
Enthusiasm for staying at the forefront of technology trends and a commitment to continuous learning, particularly in the fields of renewable energy and sustainable technologies
Excellent written and verbal communication skills, with the ability to collaborate effectively with stakeholders from different functional areas
Maintain the values of the team which include engineering excellence, curiosity, a bias for action, self-awareness, inclusivity, and vulnerability
