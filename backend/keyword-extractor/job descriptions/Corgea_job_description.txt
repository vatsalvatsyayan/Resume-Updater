About the job

Company Overview

Corgea is a Y-Combinator backed AI AppSec engineer that automatically finds, triages and fixes your insecure code. We’re building the next generation of AI-powered SAST. We work with large enterprises that have complex ecosystems to increase their application security.

Job Description

We are looking for a Software Engineer who is passionate about building secure and scalable systems. This is a remote position based in the US. The ideal candidate would be in the San Francisco Bay Area. We’re open to applicants living in California.

What You'll Be Doing

Talk with customers to understand their needs and provide technical support.
Work on AI-driven features and prompt engineering tasks.


Who Are You


1+ years of professional software development experience.
Experience with AI and prompt engineering is highly desirable.
Someone who values pragmatism and simplicity.
Demonstrated understanding of computer science fundamentals.
Self-directed and comfortable working autonomously.


Why you should apply
