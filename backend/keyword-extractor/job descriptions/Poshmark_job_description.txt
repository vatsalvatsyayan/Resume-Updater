Poshmark is a leading fashion resale marketplace powered by a vibrant, highly engaged community of buyers and sellers and real-time social experiences. Founded by Manish Chandra, Chetan Pungaliya, Gautam Golwala, and Tracy Sun, Poshmark began as a vision to revolutionize the shopping experience through technology. With the launch of the iPhone 4, our founders saw a future where technology could reinvent shopping by connecting and empowering everyday people to shop and sell from the palm of their hands. In turn, they created an end-to-end fashion resale marketplace inside an easy-to-use mobile app with three guiding principles: buying and selling on Poshmark should be simple, social, and sustainable.
