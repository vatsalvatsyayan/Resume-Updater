About the job

Unleash your potential as a software engineer and attain exceptional developer skills. On our Graduate Developer Programme, you will learn how to deliver impactful software that solves Maven's most diverse problems, from low-latency software to large scale data analysis using Python, Modern C++ and C#. You’ll achieve this through a combination of classroom sessions, real world projects and team rotations to broaden your experience working alongside some of the industry’s best developers.

We are looking for final year applicants from any STEM background with a particular interest in building highly performant systems to join our Graduate Developer Programme. The first 10 weeks of the programme will be based in the London office, where you will complete programming training led by Maven experts.

What we are looking for:


Familiarity with Python, C++ or C#
Strong analytical skills with the ability to solve problems creatively 
Strong communication skills and drive to work collaboratively 
Self motivated and enthusiasm to learn new technologies
The right to work in the US



What we offer:


A great engineering environment whereby technology is key to our success
Direct exposure to and training from expert developers
Opportunity to design and develop industry strength solutions within 3 months of your training programme
Informal, friendly and highly rewarding culture with a relaxed dress code and frequent social events
Fast growing global firm where your work will have a significant impact
Extensive support network throughout your training programme



Base Salary Range: $120,000-$140,000
