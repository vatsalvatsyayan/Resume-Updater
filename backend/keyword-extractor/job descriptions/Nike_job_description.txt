About the job

Who We Are Looking For

Nike is looking for an early-career software engineer eager to grow into a full-stack developer. You bring foundational knowledge of coding and architecture, with experience in small projects and a hunger to tackle complex systems. You’re curious, collaborative, and motivated by the impact your work can have on athletes and consumers. You value learning, embrace agile practices, and take ownership of quality through automated testing. You’re ready to contribute ideas, ask thoughtful questions, and evolve your skills in a dynamic environment.

Key Qualifications


Open to work in Beaverton, OR
0–2 years of software engineering experience
Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field. Will accept any suitable combination of education, experience and training
Strong technical and non-technical communication skills
Ability to engage in architectural discussions and apply a learner’s mindset


Preferred Technical Skills


Languages: JavaScript, TypeScript, Python
Frameworks: React, Express, Apollo
Platforms: Docker, AWS, PostgreSQL


What You’ll Work On

You’ll help build and evolve technology experiences that power Nike’s innovation and sports science initiatives. From designing full-stack applications to collaborating on machine learning workflows, you’ll play a key role in creating athlete-facing solutions.

Responsibilities


Partner with senior engineers to design and implement scalable, secure applications
Contribute to web UI development using modular, reactive frameworks
Engage in architectural discussions and provide constructive feedback
Practice DevOps principles for continuous integration and deployment
Collaborate with cross-functional teams including designers, researchers, and data scientists
Review peer code and share insights to strengthen team output
Support the evolution of engineering environments and tools
