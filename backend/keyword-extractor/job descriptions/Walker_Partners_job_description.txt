About the job

Company Description

 

Walker Partners focuses on innovative consulting strategies, leveraging partnerships and acquisitions to drive business transformation and growth. The company specializes in reorganization, principal consulting, and social ventures, providing tailored solutions to meet client needs. Known for creating impactful and sustainable business models, Walker Partners thrives on sound strategies and collaboration. The organization is dedicated to enabling meaningful change within a diverse range of industries.




 Role Description

 

This is a contract hybrid role for a Software Engineer based in the San Francisco Bay Area, with flexible work-from-home arrangements. The Software Engineer will be responsible for developing and maintaining software systems, focusing on back-end web development and object-oriented programming (OOP). Day-to-day tasks include coding, debugging, testing, implementing new features, and collaborating with cross-functional teams on software development projects.




 Qualifications

 

Strong understanding of Computer Science principles and software development practices: a proven builder
Proficiency in Back-End Web Development and System Engineering
Proven experience in high and low level programming languages
Exceptional problem-solving skills and attention to detail
Ability to collaborate in a hybrid work environment and adapt to a dynamic work schedule
Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field is preferred
Knowledge of emerging technologies and experience working in AI
