About the job

How You'll Help Us Keep Climbing (Overview & Key Responsibilities)

The Business and Operations Analytics Team specializes in delivering high-impact analytics solutions by harnessing data from diverse customer interaction points—web, mobile, call centers, and airport operations. The team ensures that business metrics are timely, accurate, and actionable.

As an Associate Software Engineer, you will be responsible for developing, testing, and maintaining software applications using Python. You will work closely with senior engineers and other team members to design and implement cloud-based solutions on AWS using Lambdas and API gateway. This is an excellent opportunity for a recent graduate or someone with limited professional experience to grow their skills in a supportive and collaborative environment.

Core Capabilities

Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:


Real-Time & Near-Real-Time Metrics
Design and maintain pipelines that capture and process operational and customer data with minimal latency.
Enable proactive monitoring and rapid response to business trends and anomalies.
Data Harvesting & Integration
Develop and maintain custom Python-based frameworks for scalable data ingestion and transformation.
Leverage Microsoft Fabric for orchestrating and managing data workflows across the enterprise.
Application Development
Build internal customer-facing applications using AngularJS for responsive and intuitive user interfaces.
Implement backend services using Python-based AWS Lambdas and API Gateway, ensuring scalability and performance.
Data Storage & Management
Use PostgreSQL for operational data storage and transactional workloads.
Employ Teradata for enterprise-scale analytics, historical data analysis, and reporting.
Business Intelligence & Reporting
Design and publish Power BI dashboards to visualize key business metrics.
Provide stakeholders with interactive, self-service reporting tools to support strategic and operational decisions.
 Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define, design, and ship new features.
 Automate deployment, monitoring, and management of cloud applications.


Team Culture


Collaborative: Works closely with business units, IT, and analytics teams to align solutions with strategic goals.
Innovative: Embraces modern cloud-native architectures and automation.
Customer-Centric: Prioritizes usability, reliability, and performance in all solutions.

What You Need To Succeed (Minimum Qualifications)


Consistently prioritizes safety and security of self, others, and personal data. 
Embraces diverse people, thinking, and styles. 
Possesses a high school diploma, GED, or high school equivalency. 
Is at least 18 years of age and has authorization to work in the United States.

What Will Give You a Competitive Edge (Preferred Qualifications)


 Bachelors Degree in Computer Science, Information Systems or related technical field is preferred.
 Experience in D3 PowerBI Development

Environmental sustainability

We embed sustainability in everything we do. Elevating Delta's world-class performance and service means our focus includes connecting people to a more sustainable future of travel. 

We’ve made a commitment to eliminate our climate impact from flying by evolving how we fly, what we fly, and the fuel we use to power our fleet. We are aiming to reduce our carbon emissions by 50% by 2050 and have launched a first-of-its-kind airline innovation lab to accelerate research, design and testing for a more sustainable future of air travel. We also work with our partners and suppliers to reduce their environmental impact, and we are all committed to educating and engaging our employees and customers about sustainability. 

Despite challenges, we have already achieved significant results, like reducing single-use onboard plastics by nearly 5 million pounds annually. We also saved 10 million gallons of fuel in 2022 while planning for an additional 10 million gallons in savings annually, thanks to the work of Delta's Carbon Council. This work not only serves us today but helps ensure future generations of Delta people can connect the world for our customers and communities.
